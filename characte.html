Rob Stark

King Robb Stark was the eldest son of Lord Eddard Stark of Winterfell and his wife, Lady Catelyn.

Eddard Stark

Lord Eddard Stark, also known as Ned Stark, was the head of House Stark, the Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North.
